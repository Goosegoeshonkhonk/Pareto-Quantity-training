# Pareto: Quantity Breaks & Discounts — CS Training

Internal training materials for the Customer Support team on the **Pareto: Quantity Breaks & Discounts** Shopify app.

## Overview

This repository contains the Day 1 training slide deck and supporting assets used to onboard CS agents on how the Pareto app works, including:

- How Pareto discounts differ from native Shopify discounts
- The three offer groups (Quantity Breaks, One Discount, and more)
- Campaign setup flow: product selection, quantity conditions, target customers, scheduling, and market settings
- Offer types: Quantity Breaks vs One Discount, templates, and layouts
- Advanced settings and Visual Swatches
- Troubleshooting when the widget doesn't display
